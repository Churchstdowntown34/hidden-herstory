About This Project

Made by women, for women. This project was created to spotlight the hidden figures in history whose work and lives were ignored, minimized, or stolen by men. We believe every woman deserves to be remembered.

Throughout history, women's contributions to science, art, literature, and innovation have often been erased, minimized, or attributed to men. Hidden Herstory aims to restore these women to their rightful place in our collective memory.

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and empower by highlighting women whose brilliance and determination changed the world, even when the world refused to acknowledge them.
